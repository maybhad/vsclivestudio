VDO.Ninja

Examples & Experiments

These demos explore different ways to embed, automate, and customize VDO.Ninja. Every page is optional, URL-driven, and kept separate from the core experience — so feel free to open, read the source, and remix them for your own workflows.

Most pages expect you to launch them with live push/view links or room IDs. Look for the on-page notes or top-of-file comments for the required parameters, or tweak the URL to try different behaviours.

    Source for every demo lives in the VDO.Ninja repository. Found a bug or built something cool? Contributions are welcome.
